\begin{abstract}
 The present work presents a series of reasons that show the importance of information security from the point of view of everyone involved, from the conception
of the operating system by the manufacturers, going through the development of the application until
end-user practices. For this purpose, a Quiz application is proposed that aims to
test the level of adherence to the recommended techniques and also serve as a kind of
guide to safe development.
\end{abstract}
